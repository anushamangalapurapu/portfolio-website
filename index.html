My Story

Hi, I'm Anusha Mangalapurapu — a passionate and enthusiastic problem-solver who thrives at the intersection of data, technology, and business impact.

My journey began with a curiosity to understand how data can tell powerful stories and drive smarter decisions. That curiosity quickly turned into a skill set: mastering platforms like Workday, Salesforce, Excel, Power BI, HubSpot, and SQL, and applying them to real-world challenges. I've worked on hands-on projects that transformed raw information into clear, actionable insights that empower teams to grow and perform better.

What excites me most is turning complexity into clarity — whether it's automating processes in Workday, building dynamic dashboards in Power BI, optimizing CRM workflows in Salesforce and HubSpot, or crafting efficient queries in SQL. Every project I take on is an opportunity to solve problems creatively, make data meaningful, and help organizations achieve measurable results.

I believe in continuous learning, collaboration, and approaching every challenge with energy and curiosity. My enthusiasm isn't just about using tools — it's about understanding people, processes, and the bigger picture behind every solution.

If you're looking for someone who brings both technical expertise and genuine passion to every project, you've come to the right place.
